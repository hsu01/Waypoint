# FIGMA MAKE PROMPT — WAYPOINT LANDING WEBSITE

Create a modern product landing page for **WayPoint**, a university social coordination platform.

This website should look like a polished startup landing page combined with a design portfolio case study.

The audience includes:

* University students
* Professors
* Design reviewers
* Recruiters
* Potential future users

The website should communicate both:

1. The product vision
2. The complete human-centered design process

The visual style should match the WayPoint poster exactly.

# RESEARCH SECTION

Title:
What We Learned

Subtitle:
Insights gathered from interviews and diary studies.

Use research cards.

Card 1:
Situational Friendships

Students maintain friendships while sharing a class or club but often lose contact afterward.

Card 2:
Busy Schedules

Even motivated students struggle to find time to reconnect.

Card 3:
Uncertainty

Students hesitate to reach out because they fear awkwardness or rejection.

Card 4:
Desire for Low-Pressure Interaction

Students want easier and more natural ways to stay connected.

Include simple visual charts.
